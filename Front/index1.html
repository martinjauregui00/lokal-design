What is Lo(k)al

Lokal is a platform dedicated to artists and designers based in the Basque Country, in witch to show or look up for artists and creative spaces focused on diferent lines. Our aim is to show and extend the net between local art and design to have a bigger development focused on a local level.

How does lo(k)al work?

In this platform you will find a catalog with diferent categories related to design and artistic disciplines and creative spaces. It is also possible to register and login to post your profile to show your work and add yourself or your company to the lokal project.

Our philosophy

We are committed to a sustainable future and local development in which artistic projects are participatory and take advantage of the talent present in this region.
